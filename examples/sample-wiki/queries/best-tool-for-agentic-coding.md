## Answer

There is no universal winner. For teams already deep in GitHub workflows,
[[github-copilot]] usually wins on ecosystem fit. For teams optimizing around
terminal-native agent conversations and long reasoning chains, [[claude-code]]
may feel stronger. The deciding factor is often not raw model quality alone, but
whether the tool's workflow and available [[context-window]] align with the job.
